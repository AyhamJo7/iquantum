# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making iquantum a welcoming, respectful project for everyone.
Contributors, maintainers, users, and community participants are expected to help
create an environment that is open, constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when words or actions have a negative impact.
- Keep disagreements technical and specific.
- Give and receive feedback in a way that helps the project improve.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear reports, reproduction steps, and context.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Personal attacks, insults, or inflammatory comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance or project boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block accounts,
or take other reasonable action when behavior violates this code.

If you need to report a concern, contact the maintainers through the repository's
private contact channel. If no private contact channel is available, open a brief
public issue asking for a maintainer contact without including sensitive details.

Reports will be reviewed in context and handled as discreetly as practical.
